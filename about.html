Meet MindCare AI

Your AI Mental Health Companion

MindCare AI is designed to be the supportive presence that understands you without judgment. Built on proven therapeutic frameworks, she can recognize distress signals, offer calming techniques, and guide you toward professional help when needed.

MindCare AI draws from Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT), Mindfulness-Based Stress Reduction (MBSR), and Acceptance & Commitment Therapy (ACT).

Our Approach

Grounded in Evidence. Built with Compassion.

We don't just build features — we build on decades of clinical research in mental health.

🧩

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)

The most evidence-supported psychotherapy. Helps identify cognitive distortions and develop healthier thinking patterns.

🔵

Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT)

Skills-based approach focusing on mindfulness, emotional regulation, distress tolerance, and interpersonal effectiveness.

🍃

Mindfulness-Based (MBSR)

Present-moment awareness techniques reduces rumination and catastrophizing, two major drivers of anxiety and depression.

🌻

Acceptance & Commitment (ACT)

Helps you stop fighting your inner experience, clarify your values, and commit to meaningful action despite discomfort.

Important Disclaimer

MindCare is an AI-powered support tool and is not a replacement for professional mental health care. MindCare AI cannot diagnose, prescribe, or provide clinical treatment. For serious mental health conditions, trauma, medication, or crisis situations, please consult a licensed mental health professional.

If you are in immediate danger, please call 112 (Emergency Services) or a mental health crisis hotline immediately.
